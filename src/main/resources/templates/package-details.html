Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 7 days before the travel date for a full refund (minus processing fees). Cancellations made 3-7 days before travel will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 3 days before travel are non-refundable.

What documents do I need to carry?

Please carry a valid ID proof (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driving License), booking confirmation, and any required travel documents. For international packages, ensure your passport and visa are valid.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not included in the package price. We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances during your trip.

Can I customize the itinerary?

Yes, we offer customization options for most packages. Please contact our customer service team to discuss your requirements and we'll create a personalized itinerary for you.

What is the difference between Standard and Luxury packages?

Standard packages include sightseeing and basic accommodation. Luxury packages include flights, premium hotel accommodation, and all meals. Both packages include professional tour guides and transportation.

How do I make payments?

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and bank transfers. You can pay the full amount upfront or opt for easy EMI options.
